🐍 Why Python?

  1. Most popular language in data science and research
  2. Readable and beginner-friendly syntax
  3. Extensive libraries for psychology research (PsychoPy, pandas, etc.)
  4. Large supportive community - help is always available
  5. Industry-standard for data analysis

🎯 Evaluation - Lab (50%)

End-of-Term Python Project

Work individually OR in teams of 2-3 students
  1. Program runs without errors and warnings (1 point)
  2. Program produces expected results (1 point)
  3. Includes meaningful test (1 point)
  4. Live modification during presentation - 5 minutes (3 points)
  5. Oral defense - answer questions about code, 1 min/question (3 points)
💡 Team work: All members receive the same grade, but each must answer at least one question!

⚠️ Evaluation - Critical Information
